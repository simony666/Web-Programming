Frequently Asked Questions



    What sizes do you offer?

    We offer a range of sizes from XS to XL. Our sizing chart can be found on our website.


    What is the material and care instructions for this item?

    The material and care instructions for this item can be found on the product page. Please follow the care instructions carefully to ensure the longevity of the item.


    Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping rates vary by location.


    How long does it take to process and ship my order?

    We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method you select during checkout.


    How do I track my order once it has been shipped?

    Once your order has been shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to track your order on our website or the carrier's website.


    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.


    Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

    Yes, we offer discounts and promotions periodically. Please sign up for our newsletter to stay up-to-date on our latest promotions.


    Can I return or exchange an item?

    Yes, we offer free returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to our return policy for more information.


    How can I contact customer support if I have further questions or issues?

    You can contact our customer support team via email, phone, or live chat. Our customer support hours are Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm.
